Lasagne recipe

Lasagna is a traditional italian dish. It is a type of flat pasta, possibly one of the oldest types of pasta.

    • Ingredients

    1. 2 tbsp olive oil, plus a little for the dish
    2. 750g lean beef mince
    3. 90g pack prosciutto
    4. half quantity of tomato sauce
    5. 200ml hot beef stock
    6. a little grated nutmeg
    7. 300g pack fresh lasagne sheets
    8. half quantity of white sauce
    9. 125g ball mozzarella, torn into thin strips

    Steps

      • Heat 2tbsp olive oil in a frying pan and cook 750g lean beef mince in two batches for about 10 mins until browned all over.

    2. Chopping the prosciutto üçñ

      • Finely chop 4 slices of prosciutto from a 90g pack, then stir through the meat mixture.

    3. Pouring the passata ü•´

      • Pour over 800g passata or half tomato sauce and 200ml hot beef stock. Add a little grated nutmeg, then season.

    4. Bringing up to the boil ‚ô®Ô∏è

      • Bring up to the boil, then simmer for 30 mins until the sauce looks rich.

    5. Heating ‚ô®Ô∏è

      • Heat oven to 180C/fan/160C gas and lightly oil an ovenproof dish(about 30 x 20cm).

    6. Spooning and covering ü•Ñ

      • Spoon one third of the meat sauce into the dish, then cover with some fresh lasagne sheets from a 300g pack. Drizzle over roughly 130g ready-made or homemade white sauce.

    7. Repeating üîÅ

      • Repeat until you have 3 layers of pasta. Cover with the remaining 390g white sauce, making sure you can't see any pasta poking through.

    8. Scattering mozzarella

      • Scatter 125g torn mozzarella over the top.

    9. Arranging

      • Arrange the rest of the prosciutto on top. Bake for 45 mins until the top is bubbling and lightly browned.
